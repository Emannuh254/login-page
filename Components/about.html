About FlipMarket

Our Story | Since 2009

Since 2009, FlipMarket has been quietly building and operating high-performance crypto infrastructure behind the scenes. What began as a small server-based experiment evolved into a scalable network of optimized mining systems designed to run continuously, securely, and efficiently.

Rather than requiring individuals to own expensive hardware, manage heat, or deal with unstable power, FlipMarket centralized the heavy lifting. Our platform allows users to participate in crypto mining by purchasing access to professionally maintained servers — what we call bots — hosted and managed by our technical team.

Empowering Users Through Shared Mining

FlipMarket bots are not software installed on your device. They are dedicated online mining servers operating 24/7 in optimized environments. When you purchase a bot on our platform, you gain access to a portion of this infrastructure, enabling you to earn from crypto mining without technical complexity.

This shared-infrastructure model makes mining accessible to users across Africa and beyond — regardless of hardware limitations, electricity costs, or technical background. You simply monitor performance while the servers do the work.

What the Bots Actually Do

Each FlipMarket bot is a remotely hosted mining instance connected to our server network. These servers mine Ethereum and other supported cryptocurrencies, dynamically allocating resources to stable and profitable networks.

The entire process is automated:

  • Mining operations run continuously on FlipMarket servers
  • Revenue generated is calculated and reflected in your account
  • Performance optimization is handled server-side
  • No downloads, GPUs, or local power consumption required

Once a bot is active, it begins generating revenue daily based on real mining activity.

How You Earn on FlipMarket

FlipMarket provides two clear earning methods, both visible and selectable when interacting with the platform.

1. Earning Through Bots

When you purchase a bot:

  • The bot generates daily mining revenue automatically
  • Earnings accumulate in your account balance
  • You must collect your earnings daily to keep your balance updated
  • Higher-tier or exclusive bots generate increased daily returns

This ensures transparency and encourages active participation in the platform.

2. Earning Through Referrals

FlipMarket rewards growth through strategic partnerships.

  • When you refer a new partner, you receive $2 USD instantly
  • There is no limit to the number of partners you can refer
  • Referral rewards are credited separately from bot earnings
  • Build passive income by growing your network

Both methods work together — users can earn through bots, referrals, or a combination of both strategies.

As your VIP status grows, so does your earning potential and platform privileges.
