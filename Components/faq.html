FAQ & Help

How can I contact support? ▼
You can contact us via our contact page.
What is FlipMarket about? ▼
FlipMarket is a platform that allows users to buy, sell, and manage digital products and bots efficiently.
How do I reset my password? ▼
Go to the login page and click on "Forgot Password" to reset it.
Can I customize the language? ▼
Yes, go to Settings > Language Preferences and choose your preferred language.
How do I delete my account? ▼
Navigate to Settings > Account Management, then follow the instructions to delete your account.
Is there a referral program? ▼
Yes! Invite friends and earn rewards when they join through your referral link.
